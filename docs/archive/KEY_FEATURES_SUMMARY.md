# 🎓 OECS LearnBoard LMS - Key Features Summary

## 🎯 **CORE LEARNING FEATURES**

### 📚 Course Management
- ✅ **Course Creation & Publishing** - Create, edit, and publish courses with rich metadata
- ✅ **Featured Courses** - Highlight courses on homepage (admin-controlled)
- ✅ **Multiple Instructors** - Assign multiple instructors per course
- ✅ **Course Organization** - Subject-based organization with lessons and materials
- ✅ **Course Discovery** - Search, filter, and browse course catalog

### 📖 Content Management
- ✅ **9 Content Types** - Text, Video, Images, PDFs, Files, Embedded Content, Slideshows, Quizzes, Assignments
- ✅ **Rich Text Editor** - Full-featured editor with formatting, images, links, tables
- ✅ **Lesson Builder** - Create structured lessons with learning objectives
- ✅ **Content Sequencing** - Order lessons and materials logically
- ✅ **Material Upload** - Drag-and-drop file uploads to Supabase Storage

## ✅ **ASSESSMENT & GRADING**

### Quizzes
- ✅ **Multiple Question Types** - Multiple choice, true/false, short answer, essay
- ✅ **Auto-Grading** - Automatic scoring for objective questions
- ✅ **Manual Grading** - Grade open-ended questions and essays
- ✅ **Multiple Attempts** - Allow students multiple quiz attempts
- ✅ **Quiz Results** - Detailed results and review for students

### Assignments
- ✅ **Assignment Creation** - Create assignments with instructions and rubrics
- ✅ **File Submissions** - Students upload files for grading
- ✅ **Grading & Feedback** - Instructors grade and provide feedback
- ✅ **Due Date Management** - Set and track assignment deadlines

### Gradebook
- ✅ **Comprehensive Grading** - Track all grades in one place
- ✅ **Weighted Grading** - Configure custom grading schemes
- ✅ **Rubric Scoring** - Use rubrics for consistent grading
- ✅ **Grade Sync** - Automatic synchronization from quizzes/assignments
- ✅ **Student Views** - Students see their own grades and progress
