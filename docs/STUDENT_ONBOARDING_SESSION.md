# New Student Onboarding Session

## Session Overview

| **Duration** | 1 Hour |
|--------------|--------|
| **Target Audience** | New students joining the platform |
| **Format** | Instructor-led with hands-on activities |
| **Prerequisites** | Student account created by administrator |

---

## Session Objectives

By the end of this session, students will be able to:

1. Log into the platform and navigate the dashboard
2. Enroll in courses and access course materials
3. Take quizzes and view results
4. Join video conferences for live sessions
5. Participate in course discussions
6. Use the AI Tutor for learning assistance
7. Track their progress and achievements

## Detailed Session Guide

### Welcome & Introduction (5 minutes)

**Instructor Script:**

> "Welcome to our Learning Management System! Today, we'll explore all the features you need to succeed in your online learning journey. This is a hands-on session, so please have your device ready and follow along with each activity."

**Key Points to Cover:**
- Brief overview of what an LMS is
- Mention the platform supports courses, quizzes, video conferencing, discussions, and AI-powered assistance
- Explain that students will earn XP points and achievements as they learn

---

### Activity 1: Logging In & Dashboard Tour (7 minutes)

**Objective:** Students successfully log in and understand the dashboard layout

#### Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **Navigate to the Login Page**
   - Open your web browser
   - Go to the platform URL provided by your instructor
   - Click "Sign In" in the top navigation bar

2. **Enter Your Credentials**
   - Enter your email address (provided by your administrator)
   - Enter your temporary password
   - Click "Sign In"

3. **Change Your Password (First Login)**
   - If prompted, create a new secure password
   - Password should be at least 8 characters with letters and numbers

4. **Explore the Dashboard**

   Point out these key elements:

   | Element | Location | Purpose |
   |---------|----------|---------|
   | **Navigation Bar** | Top of screen | Access main sections (Courses, Quizzes, Assignments) |
   | **Welcome Message** | Top center | Shows your name and role |
   | **Gamification Widget** | Dashboard area | Shows your XP, level, and streak |
   | **Announcements Banner** | Below navigation | Important platform updates |
   | **Quick Links** | Dashboard cards | Fast access to common areas |
   | **Notification Bell** | Top right | View new notifications |

**Hands-On Task:**
> "Take 2 minutes to click through the navigation menu. Identify where you would find: Courses, Quizzes, and your Profile."

---

### Activity 2: Enrolling in a Course (10 minutes)

**Objective:** Students can find and enroll in courses

#### Method 1: Browse and Enroll

1. **Access the Course Catalog**
   - Click "Courses" in the navigation menu
   - This displays all available published courses

2. **Search for a Course**
   - Use the search bar to find courses by name
   - Use filters to sort by difficulty level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)

3. **View Course Details**
   - Click on a course card to see:
     - Course description
     - Number of lessons
     - Estimated duration
     - Instructor information

4. **Enroll in the Course**
   - Click the "Enroll" button
   - Confirm your enrollment
   - You'll be redirected to "My Courses"

#### Method 2: Enroll Using an Enrollment Code

1. **Navigate to Enrollment Page**
   - Click "Enroll" in the navigation or go to `/enroll`

2. **Enter the Code**
   - Type the enrollment code provided by your instructor
   - Click "Enroll"

3. **Verify Enrollment**
   - Check "My Courses" to see your newly enrolled course

**Hands-On Task:**
> "Enroll in the practice course titled '[Instructor provides course name]' using the enrollment code: [CODE]. Verify it appears in your 'My Courses' section."

---

### Activity 3: Accessing Course Content (10 minutes)

**Objective:** Students can navigate lessons and track their progress

#### Navigating Course Content:

1. **Access Your Enrolled Course**
   - Go to "My Courses" or "My Classes"
   - Click on the course you enrolled in

2. **View Course Structure**
   - See the list of all lessons/modules
   - Note which lessons are locked (sequential courses) vs. available
   - See your progress percentage at the top

3. **Open a Lesson**
   - Click on the first available lesson
   - The lesson viewer opens with:
     - Lesson content (text, videos, documents)
     - Navigation arrows (Previous/Next lesson)
     - Completion checkbox
     - Discussion sidebar (if enabled)

4. **Interact with Lesson Content**

   | Content Type | How to Use |
   |--------------|------------|
   | **Video** | Click play, use controls for pause/seek |
   | **Documents/PDF** | Scroll to read, use download button |
   | **Interactive Video** | Answer questions that appear during playback |
   | **Code Sandbox** | Write and run code directly |
   | **Slides** | Use arrows to navigate slides |

5. **Mark Lesson as Complete**
   - After reviewing all content, check "Mark as Complete"
   - Your progress updates automatically
   - XP points are awarded!

**Hands-On Task:**
> "Open the first lesson in your enrolled course. Watch/read the content, then mark the lesson as complete. Check your progress percentage on the course page."

---

### Activity 4: Taking a Quiz (10 minutes)

**Objective:** Students can attempt quizzes and review results

#### Taking a Quiz:

1. **Find Available Quizzes**
   - Navigate to "Quizzes" in the main menu
   - Or access quizzes from within a course

2. **Start a Quiz Attempt**
   - Click on the quiz you want to take
   - Review quiz information:
     - Number of questions
     - Time limit (if any)
     - Number of attempts allowed
   - Click "Start Attempt"

3. **Answer Questions**

   | Question Type | How to Answer |
   |---------------|---------------|
   | **Multiple Choice** | Select one correct answer |
   | **True/False** | Select True or False |
   | **Short Answer** | Type your brief response |
   | **Essay** | Type detailed response in text area |

4. **Navigate During Quiz**
   - Use "Next" and "Previous" buttons
   - Question navigator shows answered/unanswered
   - Time remaining displayed (if timed)

5. **Submit Your Quiz**
   - Review your answers
   - Click "Submit Quiz"
   - Confirm submission

6. **View Results**
   - See your score immediately (for auto-graded questions)
   - Review correct answers and explanations
   - See which questions you got right/wrong

**Hands-On Task:**
> "Take the practice quiz titled '[Quiz Name]'. Complete all questions and submit. Review your results and note your score."

---

### Activity 5: Joining Video Conferences (5 minutes)

**Objective:** Students can join live video sessions

#### Joining a Conference:

1. **Find Scheduled Conferences**
   - Go to your enrolled course
   - Look for the "Live Sessions" or "Video Conferences" section
   - See list of upcoming scheduled sessions

2. **Join a Conference**
   - Click "Join" when the session is active
   - The video conference opens in:
     - A new browser tab, OR
     - An embedded video player

3. **Video Conference Features**
   - **Camera**: Toggle your video on/off
   - **Microphone**: Mute/unmute yourself
   - **Chat**: Send text messages during the session
   - **Screen Share**: (If allowed by host)
   - **Leave**: Exit the conference

4. **Best Practices**
   - Join 2-3 minutes early
   - Test your microphone and camera beforehand
   - Stay muted when not speaking
   - Use a stable internet connection

**Instructor Demo:**
> "Let me show you how the video conference interface looks. [Show the conference section in a demo course]"

**Note for Students:**
> "Video conferences are scheduled by your instructors. You'll receive notifications when a new session is scheduled."

---

### Activity 6: Using Discussions & AI Tutor (5 minutes)

**Objective:** Students can participate in discussions and use AI assistance

#### A. Course Discussions:

1. **Access Discussions**
   - Open a course
   - Click "Discussions" tab or navigate to course discussions

2. **View Discussion Threads**
   - Browse existing topics
   - Click on a thread to read posts and replies

3. **Create a New Discussion**
   - Click "New Discussion" or "Create Thread"
   - Enter a title
   - Write your post content
   - Click "Post"

4. **Reply to Discussions**
   - Open a discussion thread
   - Scroll to the reply box
   - Type your response
   - Click "Reply"

5. **Vote on Posts**
   - Use upvote/downvote buttons to indicate helpful posts

#### B. AI Tutor:

1. **Find the AI Tutor**
   - Look for the AI Tutor widget (usually a chat bubble icon)
   - Available in lessons and throughout the platform

2. **Ask Questions**
   - Type your question about the lesson content
   - The AI provides context-aware explanations
   - Use suggested questions for quick help

3. **AI Tutor Best Practices**
   - Ask specific questions
   - Request examples if you need clarification
   - The AI remembers your conversation context

**Hands-On Task:**
> "Go to the course discussions and either create a new 'Hello' thread introducing yourself, or reply to an existing welcome thread."

---

### Activity 7: Progress Tracking & Gamification (5 minutes)

**Objective:** Students understand how to track progress and earn achievements

#### Gamification Features:

1. **View Your Gamification Stats**
   - Find the Gamification Widget on your dashboard
   - See your:
     - **XP Points**: Earned through activities
     - **Current Level**: Based on total XP
     - **Daily Streak**: Consecutive days of activity

2. **How to Earn XP**

   | Activity | XP Earned |
   |----------|-----------|
   | Completing a lesson | +10-50 XP |
   | Passing a quiz | +25-100 XP |
   | Submitting an assignment | +20-50 XP |
   | Participating in discussions | +5-10 XP |
   | Daily login | +5 XP |

3. **Maintaining Your Streak**
   - Log in and complete at least one activity daily
   - Streaks boost your XP multiplier

#### Certificates:

1. **Earning Certificates**
   - Complete all required lessons in a course
   - Pass required quizzes (if applicable)
   - Certificate is automatically generated

2. **View Your Certificates**
   - Go to Profile > Certificates
   - Download as PDF
   - Share on LinkedIn
   - Copy verification link

3. **Track Course Progress**
   - Each course shows completion percentage
   - Progress bar fills as you complete lessons
   - See what's remaining to finish the course

**Hands-On Task:**
> "Check your gamification widget on the dashboard. Note your current XP and level. You should have earned XP from completing the lesson earlier!"

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Can't log in | Verify email/password, check caps lock, try password reset |
| Course not showing | Check "My Courses", ensure enrollment completed |
| Video won't play | Check internet connection, try different browser |
| Quiz won't submit | Check all required questions answered, refresh page |
| Conference won't join | Allow camera/microphone permissions in browser |
| XP not updating | Refresh the page, check if activity was completed |
