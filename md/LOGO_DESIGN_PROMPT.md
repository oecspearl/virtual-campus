# SVG Logo Design Prompt for OECS Learning Hub LMS

## Application Overview
**Name:** LearningBoard by OECS 
**Purpose:** Learning Management System (LMS) for the Organisation of Eastern Caribbean States Member States (OECS)  
**Target Audience:** Students, Instructors, Administrators across Caribbean nations  
**Industry:** Educational Technology, Professional Development, Online Learning

## Brand Identity & Colors

### Primary Color Palette (OECS Official Colors)
- **Lime Green:** `#76C74C` (Primary brand color - vibrant, energetic)
- **Navy Blue:** `#1A237E` (Secondary - professional, trustworthy)
- **Orange-Yellow:** `#FBC02D` (Accent - warm, engaging)
- **Black:** `#000000` (Text and structural elements)
- **White:** `#FFFFFF` (Background and contrast)

### Color Usage Guidelines
- Use lime green as the dominant color (represents growth, learning, Caribbean vibrancy)
- Navy blue for stability and professionalism
- Orange-yellow as accent for energy and engagement
- Ensure high contrast for accessibility
- Colors should work on both light and dark backgrounds

## Design Requirements

### Style & Aesthetic
- **Modern & Professional:** Clean, contemporary design suitable for an educational technology platform
- **Caribbean Identity:** Subtle references to Caribbean culture, unity, and regional collaboration
- **Educational Focus:** Incorporate elements that suggest learning, knowledge, growth, or connectivity
- **Scalable:** Must work at sizes from 16px (favicon) to 200px+ (header)
- **Versatile:** Should work in horizontal, vertical, and square formats

### Visual Elements to Consider
1. **Learning/Education Symbols:**
   - Open book, graduation cap, lightbulb, or knowledge tree
   - Abstract representation of growth or progress
   - Connection/networking nodes (representing collaboration)

2. **Caribbean/Regional Elements:**
   - Subtle island shapes or waves
   - Sun or starburst (connection to OECS logo elements)
   - Unity/connection lines between elements

3. **Technology/Digital Elements:**
   - Abstract geometric shapes suggesting digital connectivity
   - Circuit-like patterns or network nodes
   - Modern, tech-forward aesthetic

### Typography (Optional - if including text)
- **Font Style:** Modern, clean sans-serif
- **Text Options:** "OECS", "LearnBoard", "Learning Hub", or "MyPD"
- **Treatment:** Can be integrated into logo or separate wordmark
- **Weight:** Medium to bold for visibility

## Technical Specifications

### Format Requirements
- **Format:** SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics)
- **ViewBox:** Optimized for square or rectangular aspect ratio
- **Layers:** Organized with clear layer structure
- **Paths:** Clean, optimized vector paths
- **No Raster Elements:** Pure vector design

### Size Variations Needed
- **Favicon:** 16x16px, 32x32px (simplified version)
- **Small:** 48x48px (navbar/header)
- **Medium:** 100x100px to 200x200px (hero sections)
- **Large:** 300x300px+ (landing pages, marketing)

### File Deliverables
1. **Primary Logo:** Full-color version with all brand colors
2. **Monochrome Version:** Single-color version (lime green or navy blue) for single-color applications
3. **White Version:** Inverted for dark backgrounds
4. **Horizontal Layout:** Logo + wordmark side-by-side
5. **Icon Only:** Symbol/icon without text (for favicon, app icons)
6. **Square Version:** Optimized for square spaces (social media, app icons)

## Design Constraints

### Must Avoid
- Overly complex details that won't scale down
- Thin lines that disappear at small sizes
- Too many colors in small areas
- Text that's too small to read at reduced sizes
- Cultural stereotypes or clichés
- Elements that suggest exclusivity (should feel inclusive)

### Must Include
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Strong contrast for accessibility
- Professional appearance suitable for government/educational use
- Modern aesthetic that doesn't feel dated quickly
- Versatility for various use cases

## Usage Context

### Where Logo Will Appear
- **Navigation Bar:** Top-left corner, 48px height
- **Favicon:** Browser tab, 16-32px
- **Email Headers:** Professional communications
- **Course Certificates:** Official documents
- **Mobile App Icons:** iOS/Android app icons
- **Marketing Materials:** Brochures, presentations
- **Social Media:** Profile pictures, headers
- **Documentation:** Help pages, user guides

### Background Variations
- Light backgrounds (white, light gray)
- Dark backgrounds (navy blue, black)
- Gradient backgrounds (lime green to navy blue)
- Colored backgrounds (brand colors)
